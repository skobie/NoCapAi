About NoCap

NoCap is an advanced AI-powered application designed to help you verify and analyze content for authenticity. Whether you're checking images, videos, or other media, NoCap provides instant, accurate analysis to help you determine if content has been manipulated or is authentic.

  • AI-powered content analysis
  • Quick scan results in seconds
  • Detailed authenticity reports
  • Scan history tracking
  • Secure and private processing

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan content?

Simply upload your image or video file through the app. Our AI will analyze it and provide you with a detailed report on its authenticity within seconds.

How many free scans do I get?

New users receive 3 free scans to try out the service. After that, you can purchase additional scan tokens or earn them by playing our mini-game.

How can I get more scans?

You can purchase token packages through the app, or earn free scans by playing the built-in matching game. Each game win rewards you with additional scan credits.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We take privacy seriously. All content is processed securely, and we don't share your data with third parties. Your scan history is private and only accessible to you.

What file formats are supported?

NoCap supports most common image formats including JPG, PNG, HEIC, and WebP. Video support includes MP4 and other popular formats.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

Our AI uses state-of-the-art machine learning models trained on millions of samples. While no system is 100% perfect, NoCap provides highly accurate analysis with detailed confidence scores.

Can I view my previous scans?

Yes! All your scan results are saved in your account history. You can review them anytime from the History tab in the app.

I found a bug or have a feature request. How can I report it?

Use the Help button (question mark icon) in the app to access our feedback form. You can report bugs, suggest features, or leave a review directly from there.

Getting Started

Step 1: Create your free account using your email address.

Step 2: Upload content you want to verify using the Scan tab.

Step 3: Receive instant AI-powered analysis and authenticity reports.

Step 4: Review your scan history anytime from the History tab.
